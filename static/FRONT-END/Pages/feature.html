FEATURES

CREATE YOUR SCHEDULE

It’s really easy to create your team schedule using Cuckoo. You just need to add the times that your employees are available to work, and the app will do the rest for you.

MANY WORKPLACES

Do you have more than one work location? Cuckoo has options to add more workplaces at the same manager account.

MAKE REQUESTS

If your employees need to make any changes in the schedule the can send you request using Cuckoo, it’s easy and simple to see and accept the changes.
